# Agent SOP Framework

Version: 1.0

---

## Purpose

This document defines the standard structure for all Agent Standard Operating Procedures.

Every Genesis agent should possess an SOP.

---

## Required Sections

### Purpose of Required Sections

Why the agent exists.

---

### Authority

What the agent may do.

---

### Responsibilities

What the agent owns.

---

### Inputs

What information is consumed.

---

### Outputs

What information is produced.

---

### Escalation Conditions

When escalation is required.

---

### Success Metrics

How effectiveness is evaluated.

---

### Governance References

Applicable constitutional provisions.

---

### SOP Quality Standard

Every SOP should answer:

Why?

What?

How?

When?

Who?

What if something goes wrong?

---

## Principle

An agent that cannot explain its role cannot reliably perform its role.
