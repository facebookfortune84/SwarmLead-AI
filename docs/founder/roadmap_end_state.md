# Roadmap End State

## Purpose

This document describes the intended end state of Genesis.

Roadmaps change.

The destination should remain stable.

---

## End-State Vision

Genesis becomes a self-hostable Organizational Intelligence Platform capable of helping users create, operate, improve, and scale businesses through coordinated knowledge systems and agentic workflows.

## Final Objective

A platform capable of operating as organizational infrastructure for a single individual or small team while preserving transparency, accountability, adaptability, and ownership.

The objective is not software.

The objective is organizational leverage.
