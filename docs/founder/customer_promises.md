# Customer Promises

The relationship between Genesis and its users is founded upon trust.

The following promises are considered permanent commitments.

---

## We Will Respect Ownership

Users own their data.

Users own their workflows.

Users own their business knowledge.

Users own their intellectual property.

---

## We Will Prioritize Transparency

Users should never be forced to guess:

- what the system is doing
- why the system is acting
- how decisions were reached

---

## We Will Avoid Vendor Lock-In

Wherever practical, users should be able to export, migrate, self-host, and retain control of their systems.

---

## We Will Build For Longevity

Short-lived trends will never outweigh long-term reliability.

---

## We Will Continuously Improve

The platform will evolve.

Improvement is an ongoing responsibility.

---

## We Will Remain Accessible

Genesis exists to lower barriers rather than create new ones.

Accessibility is a design objective, not a marketing slogan.

---

## We Will Preserve Human Authority

Humans remain responsible for consequential decisions.

Automation exists to assist judgment, not replace it.
