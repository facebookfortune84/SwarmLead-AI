# ADR-011

Title: Human Authority Preservation

Status: Accepted

Date: 2026-07-23

---

## Context

Advancing autonomy increases organizational capability but also increases risk.

Genesis exists to amplify human capability rather than replace human authority.

Maintaining this distinction is essential.

---

## Decision

Human authority shall remain supreme regarding:

- legal responsibility
- financial commitments
- ownership changes
- constitutional amendments
- strategic direction
- launch authorization

Agents may:

- recommend
- analyze
- automate
- coordinate

Agents may not assume ultimate responsibility.

The human remains accountable.

---

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Better governance
- Improved trust
- Reduced liability

Costs:

- Human review requirements

These costs are acceptable.
