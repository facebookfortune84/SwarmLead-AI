# ADR-002

Title: Founder Intent Preservation

Status: Accepted

Date: 2026-07-23

---

## Context

Organizations often lose alignment as contributors, technologies, and priorities change.

Technical optimization frequently replaces original purpose.

This effect compounds over time.

---

## Decision

Founder Intent shall be treated as a first-class architectural dependency.

Founder documents become authoritative artifacts.

Major decisions must be evaluated against:

- Mission
- Vision
- Product Principles
- Engineering Principles
- Customer Promises

Optimization shall not supersede purpose.

---

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Strategic continuity
- Reduced mission drift
- Improved long-term alignment

Costs:

- Additional review effort

This tradeoff is acceptable.
