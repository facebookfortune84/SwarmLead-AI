# ADR-003

Title: Constitution-First Governance

Status: Accepted

Date: 2026-07-23

---

## Context

Most software projects rely upon undocumented norms.

As projects scale, implicit assumptions become inconsistent and difficult to enforce.

---

## Decision

Genesis adopts constitutional governance.

Governance authority originates from:

Constitution
↓
Safety Code
↓
Delegation Matrix
↓
Operational Policies
↓
Agent Permissions

All lower layers inherit authority from higher layers.

---

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Clear authority chains
- Improved auditability
- Consistent governance

Costs:

- Additional documentation

The benefits outweigh the overhead.
