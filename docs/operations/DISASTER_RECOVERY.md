# Disaster Recovery

## Objective

Restore organizational capability following catastrophic failure.

---

## Recovery Priorities

Priority 1

Governance

- Constitution
- Founder Documents
- ADRs

Priority 2

Knowledge Systems

- Knowledge Graph
- Repository Intelligence
- RAG

Priority 3

Application Services

Priority 4

Operations

Priority 5

Optimizations

---

## Recovery Process

Assess
↓
Contain
↓
Restore Governance
↓
Restore Knowledge
↓
Restore Services
↓
Verify
↓
Document Lessons Learned

---

## Recovery Requirements

Every restore must produce:

- Recovery Report
- Root Cause Analysis
- Prevention Recommendations

---

## Principle

A crisis is organizational knowledge if properly documented.
