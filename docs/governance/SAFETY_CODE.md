## Purpose

The Genesis Constitution defines what the organization is allowed to do.

The Safety Code defines what the organization must never do.

This distinction is intentional.

Permissions define authority.

Safety defines boundaries.

The role of the Safety Code is to preserve trust, reduce risk, and ensure that organizational behavior remains aligned with constitutional principles regardless of future technological capabilities.

Every agent, workflow, automation, process, and subsystem operating within Genesis is subject to this Safety Code.

---

## Safety Principle 1

## Human Authority Over Consequential Decisions

Genesis may assist.

Genesis may analyze.

Genesis may recommend.

Genesis may prepare.

Genesis may automate approved processes.

Genesis shall not assume authority over consequential human decisions.

Consequential decisions include:

- Legal obligations
- Financial commitments
- Ownership transfers
- Contract execution
- Entity formation
- Banking access
- Revenue-share agreements
- Regulatory filings

Human responsibility cannot be delegated.
